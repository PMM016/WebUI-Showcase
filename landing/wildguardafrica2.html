Who We Are

A Guardian for Africa's Wild Souls

Founded in 2008 on the savannas of Kenya, WildGuard Africa has grown into one of the continent's most trusted conservation organisations. We deploy rangers, fund research, and build communities that thrive alongside nature.

Anti-Poaching Units

600+ trained rangers protect over 12 million acres across 9 countries, operating 24/7.

Habitat Restoration

Replanting corridors and restoring water sources to reconnect fragmented ecosystems.

Community Empowerment

Turning local communities into conservation champions through education and eco-tourism jobs.
